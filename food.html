Dining Information

Important Details

  • Many restaurants are located in Taniti City and the Merriton Landing area.
  • Reservations are recommended for dinner, especially during peak tourist season.
  • Alcohol is not served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m.
  • The drinking age on Taniti is 18 years old.
  • Many restaurants and stores may be closed during national holidays.
  • U.S. dollars are widely accepted, and many businesses also accept euros and yen.
  • Most restaurants and larger stores accept major credit cards.
