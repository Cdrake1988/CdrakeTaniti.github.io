Lodging Information

Important Details

  • All types of lodging are strictly regulated and regularly inspected by the Tanitian government.
  • Most accommodations are located in or near Taniti City and the Merriton Landing area.
  • Many lodging options offer easy access to beaches, rainforest excursions, and local attractions.
  • Booking in advance is recommended, especially during peak tourist seasons and national holidays.
  • Some accommodations may offer package deals that include local tours or activities.
