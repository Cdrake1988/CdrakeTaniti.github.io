Travel to and Around Taniti

To The Island

Air Travel

The most popular way of traveling to Taniti is via air. Taniti has a single small airport that can accommodate small jet and propeller planes. Expansion of this airport is anticipated in the next few years to accommodate larger jets.

Book a Flight

Cruise Ship

It is a common occurrence that a cruise ship will dock in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night each week. For that night, those traveling by cruise ship experience the many activities available in Merriton Landing.

Around The Island

Buses

Both public and private buses are available. The public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5am to 11pm every day. Private buses run on their own schedule and cover the rest of the island.

Taxis & Rental Cars

Taxis are available for transportation within Taniti City limits. Rental cars can be rented from our local rental agency located near the airport.

Bikes

Several vendors across the island have bicycles and helmets available for rental. Exploring the island via a bicycle is highly recommended, but remember that the use of a helmet is required by law.

Walking

Both Taniti City and nearby Merriton Landing are fairly flat and very walkable. Taking a stroll around Taniti City gives you the opportunity to see all the sights and attractions.
